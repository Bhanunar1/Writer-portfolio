## Kalkrish Portfolio Blueprint

### Site Map
- Home: hero hook, CTA to work/contact, featured pieces.
- Work/Samples: 8–12 curated pieces with filters (genre/type).
- About: long bio, awards/press, speaking, downloadable press kit.
- Services: writing/editing/ghostwriting/speaking; process; rates or “contact for quote.”
- Testimonials/Clients: quotes, logos, short outcomes.
- Contact: form + direct email; socials; optional newsletter signup.
- Optional: Blog/Notes; Resources/Media kit.

### Home Wireframe (single page flow)
- Hero: portrait or literary background; hook + 1-sentence positioning; CTA primary “Work with me,” secondary “Read selected work.”
- Credibility bar: logos of publications/clients; awards badges.
- Featured work: 3–4 cards with cover image, title, genre, 1–2 line blurb, “Read.”
- About snapshot: 3–4 lines on voice/genre/mission; link to full About.
- Services teaser: bullets (essays, fiction/serials, ghostwriting, developmental editing, speaking); link to Services.
- Testimonials: 2–3 short quotes.
- Newsletter/updates: email capture (if relevant).
- Footer: contact email, socials, mini nav, legal.

### Suggested Copy (edit freely)
- Hero hook: “Kalkrish writes vivid stories and sharp essays that linger long after the last line.”
- Subhead: “Author and storyteller crafting literary fiction, essays, and commissioned narratives.”
- CTA primary: “Work with me”; Secondary: “Read selected work.”
- About opener: “Kalkrish blends lyrical prose with clear-eyed observation. His work spans literary fiction, essays on culture and identity, and commissioned storytelling for brands and publications.”
- Voice/positioning bullets: “Lyrical yet precise,” “Character-first narratives,” “Themes of belonging, memory, and place.”
- Services bullets: “Literary and narrative essays,” “Short fiction and serials,” “Ghostwriting for thought leaders,” “Developmental editing,” “Speaking/readings.”
- Testimonials intro: “Readers and editors on Kalkrish’s work.”
- Contact blurb: “For commissions, collaborations, or rights inquiries, reach out.”

### Content & Assets Checklist
- 8–12 pieces with title, short blurb, publication info, link/PDF; 3–4 featured.
- Author photos (portrait + lifestyle), cover images for samples, favicon/wordmark.
- Awards/press list; 3–5 testimonials; client/publication logos.
- One-page PDF bio/press kit; OpenGraph/social share images for Home and top pieces.
- Newsletter provider (Substack/Mailchimp) if capturing emails.
- Domain + hosting/CMS (e.g., Webflow/Framer/Squarespace/WordPress) with SSL, analytics.

### Design Cues
- Typography: literary serif for headings (e.g., Playfair/EB Garamond) + clean sans for body (Inter/Source Sans); generous whitespace.
- Color: restrained palette with one accent; high contrast.
- Imagery: consistent cover treatments; subtle textures; avoid generic stock.
- Accessibility: proper heading order, 16–18px body, focus states, alt text.
- Performance: compressed images, lazy-load, CDN, SSL.

### Palette & Type (ready-to-use)
- Palette A (calm literary): Deep navy `#0F1A2A`, Off-white `#F7F4EE`, Warm taupe `#C6A27E`, Accent copper `#C9693B`.
- Palette B (minimal modern): Charcoal `#1F1F1F`, Bone `#F6F6F2`, Mist `#DDE1E6`, Accent teal `#1EA7A1`.
- Typography pairing 1: Headings Playfair Display, body Inter; alt sans for UI: Source Sans.
- Typography pairing 2: Headings EB Garamond, body Manrope.

### Component Map (for build)

- Credibility bar: horizontal logo list with grayscale treatment.
- Work grid: cards with cover, title, genre tags, short blurb, link; filter chips (genre/type).
- Feature story block: larger highlight card with pull-quote overlay.
- About block: image + text split; “Download press kit” button.
