About

Short Bio

I'm Kal Krish (real name Narala Bhanu Prakash), a storyteller who treats imagination as a weapon and writing as an escape. I come from a modest background and built my creative world through struggle, silence, and observing human emotions.

I write dark fiction, psychological thrillers, emotional dramas, and fantasy worlds filled with heart and danger.
